About me:

A wearer of many hats who loves to solve problems and empower people to be their best. A strategic thinker who is always looking for ways to make things more efficient while maintaining a high level of quality.

I enjoy challenges and always learning. I make software tools that enable employees to do what humans do, and computers to do what computers do. With a focus on ensuring the software does not hinder or slow down the user.

In my spare time I enjoy being with family and friends, reading, and gardening. I have an interest in tropical fruiting plants and am crazy about mangos.
